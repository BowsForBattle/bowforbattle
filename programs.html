Programs

Built around consistency, safety, and camaraderie.

Programs are designed to create reliable spaces where veterans can practice archery, reconnect outdoors, and build trust with peers.

Program Pillars

Equipment Access

Bows, safety gear, and foundational support so veterans can begin confidently.

Outdoor Events

Range days and field events that combine focus training with healthy community time.

Mentorship

Guidance from peers and leaders to help sustain progress beyond a single event.

Program Principles

  • Stance: build stability before action.
  • Breath: regulate stress and sharpen attention.
  • Focus: stay present with intentional movement.
  • Patience: prioritize discipline and repetition.
  • Precision: reinforce confidence through control.
